FAQ

Common questions

Do you charge for site surveys?

No — site surveys within Nairobi are free and carry no obligation. For upcountry sites we may ask for transport reimbursement, refundable against the project.

Can I view my CCTV cameras on my phone?

Yes. Every CCTV installation includes free remote-viewing setup on your smartphone via the Hik-Connect app, so you can watch live and playback footage from anywhere.

Do your products come with warranty?

Yes — all equipment is genuine and carries the manufacturer's warranty (typically 12 months). Our workmanship is separately guaranteed.

Do you install outside Nairobi?

We install countrywide. Transport and accommodation for upcountry projects are itemised transparently in your quote.

How do I pay?

M-Pesa, bank transfer or cheque. Typical terms are a deposit on order with balance on commissioning; corporate and tender terms are negotiable.
