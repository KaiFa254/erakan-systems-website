About Erakan Systems Ltd

A Kenyan systems engineering & ICT contractor with one promise: solutions that are long-lasting, cost-effective and reliable.

Who We Are

Installers, integrators and long-term partners

Erakan Systems Limited's primary business is the installation and sale of security and ICT systems: structured cabling, electrical power and lighting installations, wiring, and alternative power sources — supported by professional maintenance and consultancy services.

Our portfolio covers CCTV solutions, electric fencing, alarms, access control, time & attendance systems, fibre and UTP cabling, wireless networks, IP telephony, lighting, domestic and commercial wiring, power inverters, solar power, standby generators and UPS backup.

We supply the best products and stand behind our installations with maintenance contracts and service level agreements. Our highly experienced professional team strives to exceed customer expectations and to uphold the highest standards of professionalism and integrity on every job.
